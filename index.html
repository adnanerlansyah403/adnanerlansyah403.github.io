What's this anime?

Attack on Titan (Japanese: Shingeki no Kyojin) is perhaps one of the greatest TV shows ever made. It is undoubtedly one of the most popular anime of all time, and will likely go down in history as an all-time classic. Attack on Titan made it’s debut in 2013 and was a smash hit not only in Asia but in the west too, because of the incredible combat scenes, the gut wrenching animations and of course the charming characters..

All Question

Who And Where Is The Ninth Titan?

When Ymir Fritz, the first Eldian to get the power of the Titans, died, her spirit broke into nine pieces, and Nine Titan powers were passed on to her descendants. These were the intelligent Titans who had a special, unique power. In the anime, only eight of the nine titans have been revealed, namely the Founding, Attack, Cart, Armored, Female, Beast, Jaw, and Colossal Titans. The ninth Titan is yet to be shown, but it is most certainly going to be the Warhammer Titan.

The Warhammer Titan has the ability to form weapons from its body, and in the manga, is in possession of Eren Jaeger. It is very likely that like season 3, season 4 will be released as a two-part final season. If so, the show will most likely introduce the Warhammer Titan in the first half of season, and leave the second half to depict the war between Eldia and Marley.

Will Any Of The Main Characters Die?

If there's one thing fans can count on Attack on Titan for, it's for gruesome deaths. But will any of our beloved main characters be dying in the final season? Well, since Eren and Armin are Titans, they can only live for about 13 years before dying, due to the Curse of Ymir. Eren, in particular, has a high chance of dying, since the powers of the Founding Titan (to control Titans and humans) is wasted on him. Only someone with royal Eldian Blood can fully control the power, so there's a possibility he will allow someone with royal blood to eat him and gain his powers, maybe Historia.

Both in the Japanese and English dubs of the show, Armin is the narrator of the show, and hence it might be unlikely that Armin is going to die since the writers wouldn't want the show to be narrated by a dead character, as it makes no sense, so he seems to be quite safe at the moment.

Will Annie Wake Up?

In season 1, after Eren and Annie fight and Eren emerges victorious, Annie encases herself in the hardened Titan skin to avoid being interrogated by the Scouts. Squad Commander Hange puts her in a heavily guarded underground prison inside Wall Rose, in case she ever wakes up. But the question is, will she?

She was part of Bertholdt, Reiner, and Marcel's group, who were trying to bring Eren back to Marley to take his powers, led by Zeke, the Beast Titan. There's a good chance that Zeke and Reiner will come back to rescue them, maybe even with reinforcements from Marley. Plus, the writers would probably not go with sidelining one of the Nine Titans, as they're very important to the story.

Will The Ackermans Play A Bigger Role?

In season 3 part 1, fans found out that the Ackerman clan was one of the noble families that were exempt from the powers of the Founding Titan, ie, the memory-wiping power of the Founding Titan did not work on those with Ackerman blood. Both Mikasa and Levi Ackerman seem to have abilities beyond the capabilities of a normal human, like Levi when he fought the Beast Titan.

Even Mikasa seemed to have great strength when she stabbed and killed the robber easily when she was 9 years old, and not to mention, has fighting abilities that surpass those of the rest of the Scouts. Anime fans are wondering if there is a special reason why members of the Ackerman clan have such superhuman abilities and if they will be revealed in the upcoming final season.

How Did Eren Kruger Know About Mikasa And Armin?

Before Mikasa and Armin are even born, Eren Kruger, who gives Grisha Jaeger the powers of the Attack Titan, tells him he must do what he says if he wants to save Mikasa and Armin. Fans were left wondering how Eren Kruger knew these names, especially since he says he doesn't know who they are when Grisha questions him about it.

We know the Ymir Titans can sometimes see the memories of their predecessors, like when Eren experiences his father's memories, so perhaps is it possible that they can see the memories of their successors? Eren Jaeger goes on to inherit Eren Kruger's powers, so perhaps Kruger was actually seeing his successor's memories before they even happened? It's an interesting concept, and a mind-blowing one if it turns out to be true. Fans will just have to wait and see what happens.
